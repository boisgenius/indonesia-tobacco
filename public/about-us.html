The working model is simple: receive the requirement, match the source, confirm the order path.

01

Requirement received. Buyer shares product type, specification, target quantity, packing, market, and timeline.

02

Supplier matched. We review relevant sub-brands, partner factories, and supply chain resources.

03

Quotation and samples. We coordinate price references, samples, technical details, and commercial conditions.

04

Order confirmed. Buyer and supplier align on specification, quantity, payment, packing, and production schedule.

05

Production and export. We follow up on production communication, shipment coordination, and export-related support.

For sourcing requests, send product details and the expected order profile before asking for final quotation.
